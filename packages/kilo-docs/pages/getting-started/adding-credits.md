# Adding More VCP Credits

Once you've used any initial free VCP Credits, you can easily add more:

- Subscribe to the [VCP Pass](https://VCP.ai/features/VCP-pass), the most cost effective way to add credits.
- Purchase additional credits as a one-time transaction.
- Enable automatic top-up, which purchases additional credits when your balance is below $5.

These options are available to purchase from your [personal profile page](https://github.com/DerstedtCasper/vcp-code-2.0/profile).

You can also use subscriptions or credits you may have purchased directly with an AI provider by adding your keys on the [Bring your own Key (BYOK)](https://github.com/DerstedtCasper/vcp-code-2.0/byok) settings screen. For setup details and supported providers, see [AI Providers documentation](/docs/ai-providers). If your provider is not yet supported, you can also [directly connect your provider](/docs/getting-started/setup-authentication) in the extension and CLI.

## Transparent Pricing

At VCP Code, we believe in complete pricing transparency:

- Our pricing matches the model provider's API rates exactly
- We don't take any commission or markup.
- $1 you give us becomes $1 of VCP credits
- We debit your VCP credits exactly what the provider charges us in dollars
- You only pay for what you use with no hidden fees
